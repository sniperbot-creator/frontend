Operational Access & Usage Policy

Sniping Tool

System Integrity & Controlled Access

The sniping tool operates within a high-frequency, low-latency execution environment. Due to the nature of blockchain congestion, MEV competition, and execution timing, the system is intentionally restricted, monitored, and access-controlled.

This tool is not designed for casual, unattended, or abusive usage.

To preserve execution quality and platform stability, access is limited to users who meet operational requirements and demonstrate active engagement during use.

Active Monitoring Requirement

While the sniping tool is running, continuous user monitoring is required.

The system:

  • Executes time-sensitive actions
  • Interacts directly with live liquidity and mempool conditions
  • Allocates priority backend resources per active session

Unattended execution, misuse, or interruption of the tool can negatively impact:

  • Transaction routing
  • Execution accuracy
  • Overall system performance

As a result, sessions are tracked, rate-limited, and evaluated in real time.

Access Commitment & Abuse Prevention

To prevent misuse, spam activity, and automated farming behavior, the platform enforces a strict access commitment requirement.

This requirement exists to:

  • Filter unserious or exploitative usage
  • Protect shared infrastructure from overload
  • Ensure fair allocation of execution resources
  • Maintain consistent performance for compliant users

Access to the sniping tool is therefore gated behind a 2 SOL operational commitment, required prior to activation.

This commitment is a system control mechanism, not a product price, not a profit claim, and not a trading requirement.

Fee Structure Transparency

All executions performed through the sniping tool are subject to clearly defined fees:

  • Axiom Protocol Fee — 1%
    Applied directly by Axiom for execution routing, transaction handling, and protocol-level infrastructure. This fee is external and not controlled by us.
  • Platform Service Fee — 1%
    Applied to support:
    • Backend infrastructure & monitoring
    • Execution stability
    • Tool development, maintenance, and updates

Fees are applied only on executed transactions. There are no hidden charges, no stacked fees, and no manipulation of trade outcomes.

Strict Usage Standards

The sniping tool operates under zero-tolerance misuse rules.

The following are prohibited:

  • Interruption or tampering with execution flow
  • Attempted automation loops outside permitted usage
  • Exploitation of system timing or rate limits
  • Misrepresentation of the tool's purpose or behavior

Violations may result in immediate restriction of access.

No Profit Guarantee Statement

The sniping tool is a technical utility, not a financial product.

We do not:

  • Guarantee profits
  • Control token price movements
  • Override market conditions
  • Eliminate slippage, failed transactions, or losses

All outcomes are dictated by network conditions, liquidity, competition, and user configuration.

Final Acknowledgment

By accessing the sniping tool, users acknowledge that:

  • They understand the risks of automated execution
  • They accept the system's strict operational controls
  • They agree to monitored usage and access requirements
  • They use the tool at their own discretion
